Unlocking the Full Potential of Cell Therapy

We are a preclinical biotechnology startup out of Salt Lake City. We are developing novel myeloid based cell therapies designed to treat solid tumors like Glioblastoma. We are currently operating in stealth, but we look forward to sharing more about our technology in the future.

Our Approach

While in stealth, we are focused on building a robust scientific platform to overcome the critical challenges in cell therapy today.

Targeted Engineering

Developing novel platform technologies to precisely engineer cells for enhanced safety and efficacy.

Patient-Focused Science

Our research is driven by a deep understanding of disease biology and the needs of the patients we aim to serve.

Scalable Manufacturing

Investing early in robust and scalable manufacturing processes to ensure future patient access.

Our Team
